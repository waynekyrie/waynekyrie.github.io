We present <strong>BrickSim</strong>, the first real-time physics-based simulator for interlocking brick assemblies.
BrickSim enables high-fidelity simulation of assembly, disassembly, and structural collapse in real time. 
It also integrates seamlessly with a wide range of robotic platforms.
By bringing complex physical interactions into the virtual world, BrickSim significantly lowers the barrier for studying dexterous, contact-rich, and long-horizon robotic manipulation.
