Ruixuan Liu

I am a PhD student in the Robotics Institute, part of the School of Computer Science, at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Prof. Changliu Liu (Intelligent Control Lab). My research focuses on robot learning and control, generative manufacturing, human-robot collaboration, and robot safety.

I received my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a minor in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon University. During my undergrad, I worked in the AirLab led by Prof. Sebastian Scherer. My work focused on sensor fusion and 3D reconstruction for building structure inspection.
